About This Event

The Community Health Fair is Peace League Africa's flagship health outreach event, bringing free medical services and wellness education to communities that lack access to quality health care. Our 2026 fair in Kigali expects to serve over 2,000 community members with free screenings, immunizations, and health education.

In partnership with the Rwanda Ministry of Health and local health centers, we provide comprehensive health services including blood pressure and diabetes screenings, HIV testing and counseling, child immunizations, nutrition assessments, and mental health first aid. Community health workers are on-site to connect attendees with ongoing care.

No appointment necessary. Walk-ins welcome. All services are completely free. Transportation assistance available for elderly and disabled attendees.
