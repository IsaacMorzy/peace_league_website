About Peace League Africa

Our Mission

Peace League Africa is dedicated to fostering peace, understanding, and sustainable development across the African continent. We believe that through education, community engagement, and compassion, we can create lasting positive change.

Our History

Founded with a vision of unity, Peace League Africa has grown from a small grassroots movement into a continental force for good. Our programs span multiple countries and touch the lives of thousands each year.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We lead with empathy in all our interactions
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and accountability
  • Innovation: We seek creative solutions to complex challenges
  • Sustainability: We build programs that last
  • Inclusivity: We welcome all who share our vision
