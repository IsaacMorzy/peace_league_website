About Peace League Africa

From a single peace workshop in 2015 to a movement spanning 12 countries — we replace division with dialogue, poverty with opportunity, and despair with hope.

❤ 🕊 ❤ 🕊 ❤ 🕊 ❤

Our Mission

Peace League Africa exists because conflict steals futures — and peace can restore them. We work alongside communities in East Africa and the Great Lakes region to replace division with dialogue, poverty with opportunity, and despair with hope.

Our History

What began in 2015 as a single peace workshop in a Nairobi community hall has grown into a movement spanning 12 African countries. Today, our programs reach more than 50,000 people annually — from conflict-resolution classrooms in Kampala to reconciliation dialogues in Kigali and clean-water initiatives in rural Kenya.

Our Values

Compassion

Every child, every mother, every elder we serve deserves to be seen, heard, and valued — not as a beneficiary, but as family.

Integrity

95% of donations go directly to programs. Our books are open, our decisions are transparent, and our promises are kept.

Innovation

From mobile-money giving platforms to community radio peace programs, we meet challenges with African creativity — not imported templates.

Sustainability

We do not just build wells; we train local water committees. We do not just teach peace; we train teachers to carry the work forward.

Inclusivity

Whether you are a farmer in Goma, a student in Nairobi, or a donor in London — if you believe in peace, you belong here.

Our Journey

From a single room in Nairobi to 12 countries — every milestone brought us closer to a more peaceful Africa.

2015

First Peace Workshop

A single peace workshop in a Nairobi community hall — 47 people, one room, and a vision that would change thousands of lives.

2017

Expansion to Uganda

Our first cross-border program launched in Kampala, training teachers in conflict resolution techniques for post-conflict classrooms.

2018

Clean Water Initiative

Began drilling wells in Turkana, Kenya, reaching 12 remote communities with clean water access for the first time.

2019

Youth Leadership Program

Launched regional youth peace summits bringing together 500 young leaders from 8 countries for dialogue and action planning.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Pivoted to deliver emergency food aid and health education across 5 countries, reaching 20,000+ families during the pandemic.

2021

12-Country Reach

Expanded operations to all 12 countries across East Africa and the Great Lakes region. 50+ staff and 1,000+ volunteers strong.

2023

50,000 Lives Impacted

Crossed the milestone of 50,000 lives directly impacted through peace education, healthcare, water, and youth programs.

2025

Peace Built Here

Launched the Peace League Africa digital platform, connecting donors directly with communities and bringing transparency to every dollar.
