About Peace League Africa

Our Mission

Peace League Africa exists because conflict steals futures — and peace can restore them. We work alongside communities in East Africa and the Great Lakes region to replace division with dialogue, poverty with opportunity, and despair with hope.

Our History

What began in 2015 as a single peace workshop in a Nairobi community hall has grown into a movement spanning 12 African countries. Today, our programs reach more than 50,000 people annually — from conflict-resolution classrooms in Kampala to reconciliation dialogues in Kigali and clean-water initiatives in rural Kenya.

Our Values

Compassion

Every child, every mother, every elder we serve deserves to be seen, heard, and valued — not as a beneficiary, but as family.

Integrity

95% of donations go directly to programs. Our books are open, our decisions are transparent, and our promises are kept.

Innovation

From mobile-money giving platforms to community radio peace programs, we meet challenges with African creativity — not imported templates.

Sustainability

We do not just build wells; we train local water committees. We do not just teach peace; we train teachers to carry the work forward.

Inclusivity

Whether you are a farmer in Goma, a student in Nairobi, or a donor in London — if you believe in peace, you belong here.
